# Character Images Guide

## Required Images

Place PNG images in this folder with these exact names:

1. **spiderman.png** - Spider-Man (for landing page)
2. **kim-possible.png** - Kim Possible (for Step 1)
3. **thanos.png** - Thanos (for Step 2, left side)
4. **peppa-pig.png** - Peppa Pig (for Step 2, right side)
5. **black-panther.png** - Black Panther (for Step 3, left side)
6. **tom-jerry.png** - Tom & Jerry (for Step 3, right side)
7. **olaf-sven.png** - Olaf & Sven from Frozen (for Step 4)
8. **captain-america.png** - Captain America Shield (for Step 5)
9. **sns-logo.png** - SNS Institutions logo
10. **sns-academy-logo.png** - SNS Academy logo

## Image Specifications

- **Format**: PNG (with transparent background preferred)
- **Size**: 200-400px width recommended
- **Quality**: High resolution for print quality
- **Aspect ratio**: Square or portrait orientation works best

## Where to Find Images

### Option 1: Use Free Stock Images

- **Unsplash**: https://unsplash.com/
- **Pexels**: https://www.pexels.com/
- **Pixabay**: https://pixabay.com/

### Option 2: Use Character Illustrations

- Search for "character illustration PNG" on Google Images
- Filter by "Labeled for reuse" or use royalty-free sources
- Download and rename files accordingly

### Option 3: Create Simple Illustrations

- Use Canva (free): https://www.canva.com/
- Use tools like Figma or Adobe Illustrator
- Keep designs simple and colorful

After adding images, the app will automatically:

## SNS Logos

For the SNS logos:

- Contact your institution's marketing/branding team
- Request official logo files in PNG format
- Ensure you have permission to use them
